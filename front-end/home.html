Welcome!

SkillPath Navigator is designed to help you discover your ideal career path based on your skills, interests, and personality. You will take an interactive assessment that evaluates different aspects of your abilities and preferences. Based on your results, we’ll provide personalized career suggestions, recommended courses, and projects to help you grow in your chosen field.
Take a short assessment (30 questions across 6 categories). We’ll score your strengths and suggest careers, with a few courses and project ideas to help you start.
